Startup Ideas

Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) for Assistive Technology:

Develop a brain-computer interface (BCI) system designed to assist individuals with disabilities in controlling external devices using their brain signals. This technology could enable people with paralysis or motor impairments to communicate, interact with their environment, and regain independence.

Personalized Health and Wellness Platform:

Develop a comprehensive health and wellness platform that utilizes data analytics, wearable devices, and personalized coaching to help individuals achieve their fitness and wellness goals. Offer features such as customized workout plans, nutritional guidance, progress tracking, and access to virtual health coaches.
